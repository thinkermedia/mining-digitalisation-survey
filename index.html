Digitalisation & Risk in the Global Mining Industry
Primary Research Survey · 2026

The digitalisation of the world has been underway for some time — however with mixed success in the mining industry. The stories and evidence of wasted capex on digital projects is well known, yet the imperative to make operations and companies digital-facing remains.

Today's mining industry carries the juxtaposition of digitalisation risk and the opportunity to better manage existing risk by better using digitalisation tools. It is a tricky landscape, but the prizes are real for those brave and smart enough to successfully navigate it.

As a digitalisation professional, we'd like to get your expert opinion on how well miners are digitising operations — and how digitalisation tools are being used to better manage mining risk.

About this research: This survey is conducted by Thinker Media Group as part of ongoing primary research into digitalisation trends and risk management in the global mining sector. All responses are fully anonymous and confidential. The survey takes approximately 8–10 minutes.
